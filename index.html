about us

Suma Blessing Foundation was established in May 2021 in Delhi, the capital city of India. It is a Non - Profit organization that works for the development of our country. It was founded by Basant Jena, a young Businessman & Entrepreneur.

The motive of this foundation is for the development of the country in various segments like in the field of Research and Development (R&D), Education, Health & Nutrition, Skill Development, Rural Development, Environmental, Women Empowerment, Religious Activities, etc.
